ABOUT ME


Get To Know Me A Little | October 2021


You could say that I’m an aspiring writer, someone interested in reading. Life seems to be wonderful in books and on screen. Stories have always opened my mind to do more and to live happily with a purpose. My name is Airamin C. Bautista, and this is my story.


Growing up in a less privileged community here in Antonio Isip Street, Paco, Manila has been challenging but has also helped me realize the value of education. Life often feels chaotic and uncertain. We experienced a lot of hardships but My family and I have conquered it as one.


Today, I’m a 20-year old junior student taking up Bachelor of Scicence in Computer Science at Technological University of the Philippines-Manila. I dream to work in an IT company as a data analyst and if ever luck is on my side, a project manager in the future.
